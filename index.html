  2. HEIGHT-MEASURING MACHINE

  3. CARDIACOTECH
    The risks of heart-attack and other ailments related to the heart are very common in India and other countries. In many a times, incorrect treatment is done due to incorrect diagnosis or delayed treatment. To determine heart ailments, the most effective procedure is to check the electrocardiogram (ECG) of a person and reading an ECG correctly is very much important. So, keeping in view the increasing rate of heart ailments and to help people in curing the heart-ailments, we have to planned to launch an open-source free software named “Cardiacotech”, that can easily draw out inference and come out with the correct heart-condition of a human being, within a matter of seconds, just by scanning one’s ECG report. Team members: Procheta Bhattacharyya, Piyasi Das, Jotirmoy Bhowmik 6th runners-up at Hult Prize on-campus 2020, third at Envisage 2020, B-Plan competition at Scintillations 2020, IIEST Shibpur.
